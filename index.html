About Zekri

A Legacy of Trust & Precision

Since 2017, Zekri Dental Clinic has grown into the largest dental clinic in Africa — and Algeria's most trusted name in comprehensive dental care.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Ouled Yaïch, Blida spans about 2,000 m² equipped with the latest European dental technology.
